### What we need to draw a line on the grid? 

How we move to a certain point in the grid ?

{: .fragment} 
moveTo(X, Y);

How we draw a path for a line - from a point to another?

{: .fragment} 
lineTo(X, Y);

How we draw the actual line  ?

{: .fragment} 
stroke();
